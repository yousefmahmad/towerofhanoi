Find a New Home

Help Tuan find a new home for his large family! He's moving to Hanoi looking to build a new tower for his large family. In order to do this, he needs your help. Take apart the tower from it's current location and move it to the far left board. You have to follow these rules though sot that you don't break any of his family's belongings:

  • You can't stack larger pieces of the tower on top of smaller pieces
